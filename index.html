Strengthening the Rochester Community

Community Health Engagement Partners is an organization that empowers communities to live healthier lives through awareness and education. The Community Health Engagement Partners organize and evaluate faith-based activities in Rochester, as well as provide better customer service and support to the entire African-American community, its activities and programs.


Mission

To build, strengthen and sustain healthy communities by providing education and the tools that people can use to empower themselves.
